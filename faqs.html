Help Centre

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know about our products, shipping, warranty and support.

Find answers to the most common questions about our products and services. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us directly.

General Questions

What products does ArcBlade Works offer?

We specialise in manufacturing high-quality circular saws, including professional-grade models, compact saws and accessories such as replacement blades.

Are products available for international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping. Please check our shipping page for details on delivery locations and rates.

What warranty is provided with your tools?

Our tools come with a 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. For more information, please visit our warranty page.

Product Support

How can I get technical support for my product?

If you need technical assistance, contact our support team via our Contact page. Our experts will guide you through troubleshooting and solutions.

Where can I find spare parts for my circular saw?

Spare parts can be purchased through our online store. Please visit the "Parts" section of our website for more details.

Shipping and Orders

How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes 3–7 business days for domestic orders and 7–14 business days for international orders, depending on location.

Can I track my order?

Yes — once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number by email and can track its status on our website.

Returns and Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on most products. If you're not satisfied, please refer to our return policy page for how to initiate a return.

How do I exchange a product?

To exchange a product, contact our customer service team and they'll assist you through the exchange process.
