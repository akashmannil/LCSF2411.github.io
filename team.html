Our Story

Grown from a single workshop.

Founded in 1998, ArcBlade Works has grown from a small workshop in Sarnia, Ontario, to a trusted name in the power tool industry. Our vision of delivering high-performance circular saws and innovative power tools has been at the core of our success.

Vision

Our vision for the future.

Looking ahead, we plan to expand our product range, introduce new technologies, and continue our commitment to customer satisfaction — redefining the standards of quality in the power tool industry.
